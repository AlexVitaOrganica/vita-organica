Private Label vs. White Label — What's the Difference?

Private Label

YOU'RE HERE

A formula developed exclusively for your brand. Custom ingredients, custom ratios, custom identity.

  • Unique formula built from scratch for your brand
  • Full intellectual property ownership
  • Exclusive to you — not sold to other brands
  • Custom flavors, ingredients, and delivery formats
  • Deeper regulatory guidance and documentation
  • 8 – 12 week average timeline

White Label

A proven, pre-made formula you brand as your own. Fastest path to market — ideal for rapid launches.

  • Pre-existing, market-tested formula
  • Shared formula base (your brand, not exclusive)
  • Faster to launch — approximately 60 days
  • Lower upfront development cost
  • Limited customization options
  • Great for validating demand quickly
Full Ownership

Everything You Build Is Yours

Private label manufacturing means you walk away with exclusive rights to your formula, not a shared base that any competitor can also buy.

Proprietary Formula

Your formula is developed exclusively for your brand and locked to your account. It will never be sold or licensed to another company.

Brand Identity

Custom packaging, label design, and visual identity crafted to position your product distinctly in your market.

Custom Ingredients & Ratios

Choose your ingredient stack, potency levels, and delivery format — from novel nootropic blends to certified organic botanicals.

Full Documentation

Certificate of Analysis, stability data, ingredient sourcing records, and full regulatory documentation — all yours.

Competitive Protection

Because no other brand can access your exact formula, you build a defensible product moat that white label cannot offer.

Scalable IP Asset

A proprietary formula is a business asset. It adds real value to your brand equity, especially when raising capital or exploring acquisition.
