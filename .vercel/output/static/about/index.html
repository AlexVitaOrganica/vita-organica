About Us

We're Not Just a Manufacturer. We're Your Brand's Foundation.

Vita Organica was built for one reason: to give supplement brands the manufacturing partner they actually deserve. One that's transparent, fast, and as invested in your success as you are.

We know what it feels like to have a great product idea and nowhere reliable to take it. That's exactly why we built a different kind of operation. A U.S.-based, FDA-registered, cGMP-compliant facility that helps brands come to life.

Our Story

Vita Organica was born out of frustration with the status quo.

Starting with a commitment to clean sourcing, rigorous testing, and honest communication, we built a manufacturing operation from the ground up that we'd actually want to use ourselves.

Today, we've helped many brands launch and scale, from first-time founders with a single SKU to established brands expanding their entire product lines.

What We Stand For

Integrity in Every Step

Transparency over shortcuts

You'll always know exactly what's going into your product, where the ingredients are sourced, and what every step of the process costs. No hidden fees. No mystery ingredients.

Quality that holds up under scrutiny

Every batch is manufactured in our facility and independently third-party tested. We meet FDA standards not because we have to, but because your customers trust depends on it.

Speed without sacrifice

Our average time to market is 60 days for white label products. We've built our processes to move fast without cutting corners because in this industry, you can't afford either delay or compromise.

Partnership, not just production

We don't disappear after your first order. We stay on as your manufacturing partner, helping you iterate on formulas, scale up production, and add new SKUs as your brand grows.

What We Make

Held to the Highest Standards

From flavor-forward gummies and clean-label capsules to functional powders and specialty honey gels, we manufacture across six product formats all under one roof, all held to the same standards.

Whether you need a fully custom formulation built from scratch, or a proven white label formula you can brand as your own, we have the capabilities and the team to get you there.
