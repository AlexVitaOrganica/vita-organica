Our Design Process

From Brief to Print-Ready in Weeks

Our streamlined design workflow means you get professional, compliant packaging design without the back-and-forth of managing external agencies.

Get Started
1

Brand Discovery & Briefing

We learn about your brand story, target audience, competitive landscape, and aesthetic preferences to define a clear creative direction.

Day 1-2
2

Concept Development & Moodboarding

Our designers present 2-3 visual directions with moodboards, color palettes, and typography options so you can choose the path that feels right.

Days 3-5
3

Label Design & Iterations

Your chosen concept is refined into a full label design with front panel, back panel, Supplement Facts, and all required elements. Includes up to 3 revision rounds.

Days 5-10
4

Compliance & Regulatory Review

Our compliance team reviews every claim, ingredient listing, and disclaimer to ensure your label meets FDA and FTC requirements before it goes to print.

Days 10-12
5

Print-Ready Files & Production Handoff

Final files are prepared in print-ready formats and seamlessly handed off to our production team - no file transfers, no miscommunication, no delays.

Days 12-14
Packaging Formats

Retail-Ready Packaging for Every Product

Choose from a wide range of packaging formats. Our design team ensures your label works perfectly with every container type.

Bottles & Jars

HDPE, PET, and glass options with custom wrap-around labels, shrink sleeves, or direct printing.

Boxes & Cartons

Custom-printed folding cartons for a premium unboxing experience. Perfect for gift sets and retail displays.

Stand-Up Pouches

Resealable pouches with full-panel printing, ideal for powders, protein mixes, and bulk supplements.

Sachets & Stick Packs

Single-serve convenience packaging for on-the-go consumers. Full color printing with tear-notch options.

Shrink Sleeves

360-degree full-coverage label wraps for maximum shelf visibility and tamper-evidence.

Custom Kitting

Multi-product bundles and subscription box assembly with branded inserts, cards, and outer packaging.
